> BIO

Honours Computer Science at McGill University, Montreal.

Having worked with researchers from Copenhagen University (2023-present), the Free University of Brussels (2020), and McGill University (2022-present), I have developed research interests at the intersection of Ethics, Tech and Genetics, focusing on ethical AI in healthcare, handling genetic data, and genetic discrimination.

Through my extracurriculars, I've explored different areas of theoretical Artificial Intelligence as well as front-end development.
