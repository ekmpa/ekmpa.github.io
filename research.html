Research


My research has been mainly related to the fields of bioethics and the ethical, social and legal aspects of technologies, including on the subjects of telemedecine, genetic testing technologies, and AI. In many of these projects, my role included preliminary research, collaborator coordination, data analysis, writing, and result interpretation.

I have had the opportunity to share my research in conferences, guest lectures, media and high impact scientific journals.

My recent focus areas include:

  • Genetic Discrimination & legal work around it
  • The EU AI Act
  • The Ethical Use of Genetic Ancestry Tests in Genomics Research
